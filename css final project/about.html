Our Story

Designing furniture that stands the test of time

Our Philosophy

Founded in 2015, Urban Nest began with a simple idea: create furniture that people truly love living with. We believe good design should be accessible, sustainable, and built to last.

Quality Craftsmanship

Each piece is meticulously crafted by skilled artisans using time-honored techniques combined with modern technology.

Sustainable Materials

We source responsibly harvested wood and eco-friendly materials to minimize our environmental impact.

Thoughtful Design

Our designs balance form and function, creating pieces that are beautiful and practical for everyday life.
