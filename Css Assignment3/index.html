    About Us

    Reese's Peanut Butter Cups were created in 1928 by Harry Burnett (H.B.) Reese, a former dairy farmer and shipping foreman for Milton S. Hershey. Reese left the Hershey company to start his own candy business in his basement

    The original Reese's Cups were made with Hershey's chocolate, establishing a connection that would eventually lead to The Hershey Company acquiring the H.B. Reese Candy Company in 1963 for $23.5 million.

    Today, Reese's is the best-selling candy brand in the United States, with annual sales exceeding $2 billion. The brand has expanded to include numerous variations, including Reese's Pieces, Reese's Sticks, and seasonal shapes.
