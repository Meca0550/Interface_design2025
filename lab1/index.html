Adventure Lives Here

Ideal for fun-filled family vacations, year-round getaways, and meetings.

Welcome to Horseshoe Valley Resort, the ultimate family-friendly destination for four-season fun, located just over an hour north of Toronto and 20 minutes from Barrie, Ontario. Adventure down our resort’s 29 Alpine ski and snowboard runs, 30km of groomed Nordic trails, a dynamic terrain park, exhilarating snow tubing runs, plus fat biking and snowshoeing experiences.

Come summer, Horseshoe Resort transforms into Ontario’s lift-operated Bike Park, offering unmatched downhill biking adventures. Play a scenic round on our 18-Hole Valley Golf Course or explore our extensive outdoor offerings, including Lake Horseshoe beachfront activities, indoor and outdoor pools, slopeside fun for the kids, Treetop Trekking, Yamaha Adventures, and scenic hiking and biking trails.

Dine in our two inviting restaurants, unwind at Amba Spa or Vettä Nordic Spa, and explore more with every visit. Horseshoe Valley Resort is your year-round retreat for adventure and relaxation, promising memorable moments for everyone!
